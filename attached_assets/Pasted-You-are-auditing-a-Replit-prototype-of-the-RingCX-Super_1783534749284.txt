GROUND TRUTH — SCOPE TIERS (important; the eng baseline is narrower than the product wiki)

TIER A — COMMITTED v1 BASELINE (this is what "done" should be measured against)
A1. Live transcript for the monitored interaction — VOICE and DIGITAL.
    - Digital: renders the Customer ↔ Nova AI conversation as turns, updating live.
    - Should reuse an embedded transcript/AI-assist panel (think CCAI iframe), keyed by
      interaction/task/session context — NOT a bespoke one-off renderer.
A2. Take Over (disengage) — supervisor takes over an active Nova session and becomes a REAL
    participant (not a monitor/whisper/barge session).
    - Voice: connect supervisor first, THEN drop the AI leg (avoid dead air).
    - Digital: stop the Nova worker, attach supervisor to the existing customer task; if
      attach fails, fall back to requeue.
A3. Transfer to Queue (redirect) — take the AI off and route the interaction to a QUEUE
    (end state = queue ownership, NOT the supervisor, and NOT a specific named agent).
    - Must VALIDATE the target queue (exists/active/subaccount/permission) BEFORE stopping
      the AI; if invalid, reject before disengaging.
    - Needs a queue picker.
A4. Action ownership — Take Over and Transfer to Queue are triggered from the supervisor
    UI/action row. The transcript/AI-assist (CCAI-style) panel must NOT be the action source.
A5. Supervisor Assist — reuse the existing "View Insights" AI-assist panel for Nova
    interactions (same as human); an entry point to open/close insights while monitoring.
A6. Per-channel action model:
    - Voice: Take Over + Transfer to Queue
    - Digital live (chat/social/SMS/WhatsApp): Take Over + Transfer to Queue
    - Email (async): assign / transfer / create case (NOT live take-over)
    - The action row should be channel-aware (not a voice/digital binary).
